"hold shooting button" - time one needs to hold the shooting button in order to shoot. [seconds]
"number of teeth" - number of teeth inside the stepper motor. This determines the amount of steps a stepper motor can do
"number of teeth stepper" - number of teeth on a gear mounted on stepper 
"number of teeth turret" - number of teeth on a gear that rotates turret
"initial velocity" - initial velocity of a projectile. [m/s]
"x0" - position of a shooting point [m]
"y0" - height of a shooting point relative to the ground [m]
"dx" - horizontal distance between stepper axis and cannon axis. [mm]
"dy" - horizontal distance between stepper axis and cannon axis. [mm]
"r" - radius of a disk that is connected to the cannon
